Featured Trip Experiences

Canyon Rapids Escape

Two full days on a dramatic class III–IV river, weaving between towering canyon walls and rushing rapids. Includes one riverside camp night and a guided sunset hike.

Sunrise Family Float

Enjoy a morning of gentle class II rapids with plenty of time for wildlife watching and riverbank snacks. Perfect for families, first-timers, and relaxed scenic rafting.

Wilderness Expedition

A four-day deep canyon journey with whitewater challenge, remote campsites, and stargazing from the riverbank. Bring your sense of adventure for the ultimate rafting experience.
